Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 1, 2025

DyLexAid is a text simplification and reading assistant designed to support people with dyslexia. Your privacy is important to us. This policy outlines how we handle user data.

1. Data Collection

DyLexAid does not collect any personal data from users. We do not track you, and we do not store any of your personal information on our servers.

2. Text Processing

All text simplification and text-to-speech processing happens locally on your device. No text is uploaded or sent externally.

3. Analytics & Third Parties

DyLexAid does not use third-party analytics or advertising services. No user behavior is tracked or analyzed.

4. Children’s Privacy

DyLexAid is designed for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately.

5. Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this policy, we will update the effective date at the top of this page and include the updated version in our next app release.

6. Contact

For any questions or privacy-related concerns, please email us at janst.geo@gmail.com.
